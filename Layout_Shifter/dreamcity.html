Overview

It's time to bring out the wines and breads, and mozzarella and pasta, because we are bringing you DreamCity. This luxurious Mediterranean-themed township is barely 3 km from Hebbal Ring Road.

Stunning landscaped gardens, ornate fountains, pebbled pathways and acres of greenery make this 36-acre Mediterranean paradise the finest place to live in Bengaluru. Apartments in Casa Paradiso, a part of DreamCity, inspire its residents to celebrate life in its full glory. These ready-to-move-in homes also come with a luxurious ready-to-use clubhouse and best-in-class amenities.
